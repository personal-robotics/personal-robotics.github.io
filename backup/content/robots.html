Meet Our Humanoid: Open, Expressive, and Ready to Evolve

Standing 65 cm tall, our humanoid platform is compact, expressive, and built for real experimentation.

With 25 degrees of freedom powered by standard servo motors, she moves with surprising nuance—whether you’re testing gestures, locomotion, or interaction.

Her digital eyes aren’t just displays—they’re windows into emotion and intent. Behind them, there’s space for a camera module and the onboard brain of your choice: Raspberry Pi, Nvidia Jetson Orin, or any standard SBC.

Every part is designed for flexibility:

  • Open source firmware and control software
  • Modular hardware
  • Laser-cut, eco-friendly frame
  • Hackable from day one

She’s more than a robot. She’s a platform for learning, research, and human-robot interaction—scalable, ethical, and yours to command.
