Democratize Robotics

The age of embodied artificial intelligence is coming. But who will control it?

Today, robotics is dominated by industrial giants.

Closed systems, proprietary software, corporate ownership.

The tools that could change our lives

—help us move, see, understand, care,

are locked behind licenses and surveillance.

What should be our companions are designed as data farms.

What should be ours belongs to them.

We believe in a different future.

Robots are personal devices.

They move through our homes, watch over our families, listen to our voices, and see our faces.

They are not search engines. They are not ad platforms.

They do not belong in the hands of corporations who harvest data as a business model.

We are building the open standard for personal robotics.

A platform that is transparent, secure, and owned by the people who use it.

We are not here to build toys. We are here to turn tools into companions.

From factories to caretaking facilities, from the workplace to the living room,

we believe robotics should serve humans—not track them.

Our systems are open source. Our designs are auditable. Our environments are closed and contained by default, protecting against cyber attacks and malevolent code.

Your robot should know you—not sell you. Your data stays yours. Always.

We are reclaiming robotics from the corporations. We are democratizing robotics.

Let’s build a world where robots are trusted partners, not corporate assets.

Let’s build it together.
